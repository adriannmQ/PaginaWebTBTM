The Challenge

Educational institutions and companies needed to convert any training material into interactive, auto-correctable exams — eliminating manual effort and ensuring consistent evaluation.

They also needed real-time visibility into each student's progress, improvement areas and overall programme effectiveness, accessible from a single panel.

Our Solution

We built an AI pipeline that ingests syllabuses (PDF, DOCX, plain text) and automatically generates multiple-choice, open-ended and true/false questions calibrated to the depth and difficulty of the content.

The system grades responses using LLMs, tracks per-student progress, detects knowledge gaps and generates personalised feedback reports — all with zero human intervention after the initial upload.
