The Challenge

The client's website was a static brochure — visually polished but functionally inert. Visitors had no way to get instant answers, qualify their interest or connect with the CRM without filling out a form and waiting days for a response.

Multiple business tools (CRM, payments, CMS, analytics) were completely isolated from the website, requiring manual data entry and creating blind spots in the customer journey.

Our Solution

Bull built a modern web platform with a headless CMS at its core, connected via API to the client's entire technology stack. Every conversation, form submission and purchase flows automatically into the correct system — HubSpot, Stripe, or the ERP — with zero manual handling.

The embedded conversational AI uses a fine-tuned model trained on the company's product catalogue, FAQs and pricing logic. It qualifies leads, answers questions and triggers workflows in connected systems — acting as a 24/7 sales assistant that escalates to a human only when needed.
