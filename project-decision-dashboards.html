The Challenge

Executive teams were drowning in disconnected data: sales reports in spreadsheets, web analytics in one tool, financial data in another. No unified view meant slow, incomplete decisions.

The business needed a single source of truth that could ingest heterogeneous data in real time, surface anomalies proactively and be understood at a glance by non-technical stakeholders.

Our Solution

Moon designed the information architecture — defining the KPI hierarchy, alert logic and visual language optimised for executive cognition. Bull built the data ingestion pipelines, a real-time warehouse and a dashboard layer with sub-second query performance.

The integrated AI insight engine monitors all metrics continuously, detects anomalies using statistical models and generates natural-language recommendations that executives can act on immediately.
