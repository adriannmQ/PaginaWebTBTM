The Challenge

Creating consistent, high-quality short-form video content at scale required large editorial teams, voice talent, video editors and platform managers — an unsustainable cost structure for any media brand trying to dominate multiple channels simultaneously.

The client needed to publish daily across YouTube, TikTok and Instagram with a coherent narrative voice, without growing headcount proportionally.

Our Solution

Moon defined the content architecture — persona profiles, narrative frameworks and the editorial logic that governs topic selection, hook formulas and call-to-action patterns. Bull built the end-to-end pipeline: LLM script generation, neural text-to-speech synthesis, automated video assembly and native platform API publishing.

The system runs on a scheduler: configure once per persona, receive a daily output of fully produced and published videos with zero editorial involvement.
